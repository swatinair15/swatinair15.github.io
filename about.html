Work About Resume
I'm a Multidisciplinary Designer with a passion for crafting engaging digital experiences. My creative journey began with my bachelor's in fashion design, but I soon found myself captivated by the digital world—how design and technology come together to craft experiences that aren’t just seen but felt. That curiosity led me to graphic design, where I embraced new tools, trends, and the challenge of constantly evolving.

Wanting to dive deeper into the connection between design and technology, I pursued a Master’s in Human-Computer Interaction from Newcastle University, where I graduated with distinction. Now, as a multidisciplinary designer, I love blending aesthetics with functionality—whether through UX, motion, or visual design—to create experiences that are both intuitive and visually compelling.

I'm always curious, always learning, and always excited for new challenges.

What’s keeping me busy lately?
