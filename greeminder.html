Reducing Energy Waste Through Intelligent Design

Timeline: 4 weeks
Role: Design Strategy, User Research & Design

Design Brief

The challenge was to design a social and technical intervention that empowers users to collect data from their environment or others for fostering a sense of "living well together". The solution addresses environmental awareness and data-driven decision-making.

Solution Overview

The proposed solution was an app that encourages sustainable habits by notifying users of idle device periods, promoting energy conservation, and educating users about the environmental impact of leaving devices powered on. Key features include:

▸ Idle Device Alerts: Utilises Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) signals to detect when users are moving away from their devices, sending reminders to power down.

▸ Educational Insights: Provides real-time information on the environmental impact of idle devices to foster responsibility.

▸ Data Security: Implements robust privacy measures with transparency in data usage and user control over data sharing.

▸ Community Engagement: A feature enabling users to share ideas and activities for promoting environmental awareness.

Design Process

Design Challenges

Through background research and user interviews, it was discovered that individuals possess diverse habits, resulting in varying levels of awareness regarding the impact of energy consumption on the environment. Additionally, privacy concerns emerged as a significant issue. These findings helped in formulating key problem statements.
Problem Statement Solution
Users have different lifestyles, hence, they will have different priorities The application provides different features that users can use to customize their experience when using the application to fit their lifestyle preferences
Users may be concerned with their location data being collected The application provides the required security measures to protect users' data, and provide the users with option to share their data and reverse consent
Users may be interested in sharing experiences and ideas to promote environmental awareness The application provides a community feature where users can share ideas and activities with the community
How might we design an engaging and user-friendly mobile application that effectively raises environmental consciousness among users, encouraging them to power off electronic devices when not in use?

Evolution of the Idea

The initial concept of the app centred around notifying users on their devices being overcharged, when in case they were not around.
Initial Concept Scenario

However, further research revealed that the app's technology could not detect whether devices were overcharging. It relies on Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), which triggers a reminder when the signal strength drops below a certain threshold, assuming the user is moving away.
The app detects the robust bluetooth signal, indicating the user’s proximity to the device

The app locates that the user has moved away when the bluetooth signal is no longer detected

The app then sends a notification upon losing the bluetooth signal from the device

The user becomes aware that their device is still powered on and in idle state

The user can choose to receive another notification based on their convenience

The user powers off their device and appreciate receiving the reminder
